Act as a world-renowned full-stack software architect, AI systems engineer, revenue operations expert, B2B CRM architect, AgentMail product specialist, and Apple-level UX designer.

We are revisiting the existing **Team Training Leads** page.

The previous audit improved the B2B pipeline, urgency signals, lifecycle visibility, Revenue Agent intelligence, sorting, pipeline value, and priority actions.

However, it missed a major requirement:

**Team Training Leads must have AgentMail and lead-intelligence parity with Athlete Leads.**

A large portion of Team Training Leads are sourced through outbound research and internet scraping. This means the page is not only a B2B CRM. It is also an outbound sales operating system.

Do not invent a second outbound architecture.

Do not create parallel AgentMail logic.

Do not redesign the page.

Do not remove the improvements already implemented.

Use the existing **Athlete Leads implementation as the reference architecture** and bring all applicable intelligence, communication, outcome tracking, and AgentMail capabilities into Team Training Leads.

Preserve the differences between B2C athlete acquisition and B2B organization sales.

Every AgentMail or revenue recommendation should explain:

* Why this organization was prioritized
* Which real signals influenced the recommendation
* Why outreach is recommended now
* Which contact or role is being targeted
* Which sequence or touch is being used
* What previous communication exists
* Expected next step
* Potential downside if ignored
* Confidence, only when supported
* Which agent owns the recommendation

Do not expose prompts, hidden reasoning, or chain-of-thought.
