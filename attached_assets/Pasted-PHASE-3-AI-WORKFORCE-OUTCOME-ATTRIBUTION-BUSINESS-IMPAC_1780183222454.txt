PHASE 3 — AI WORKFORCE OUTCOME ATTRIBUTION & BUSINESS IMPACT ENGINE

Objective

Phase 1 made the AI Workforce real.

Phase 2 made the AI Workforce observable.

Phase 3 must make the AI Workforce valuable.

The goal is to move from:

“Agents performed actions”

to

“Agents produced measurable business outcomes.”

Every agent action should ultimately connect to revenue, retention, efficiency, lead generation, scheduling, or operational performance.

Do not create synthetic metrics.

All attribution must be evidence-based and traceable.

PART 12 — Success Criteria

At completion the AI Workforce platform should answer:

How much revenue did the workforce generate?

How much revenue did it recover?

How much revenue did it protect?

How many hours did it save?

What opportunities did it uncover?

Which agents create the most value?

What is the workforce ROI?

What business outcomes were directly influenced by AI?

The platform should no longer be measuring activity.

It should be measuring economic impact.

Final Goal:

Transform TrainEfficiency from an AI Workforce Management System into an AI Workforce Performance Platform where every agent is accountable for measurable business outcomes.
