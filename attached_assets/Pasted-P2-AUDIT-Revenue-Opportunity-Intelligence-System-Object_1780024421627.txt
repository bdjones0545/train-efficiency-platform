P2 AUDIT — Revenue Opportunity Intelligence System

Objective

The Attention Inbox client intelligence layer is now clean.

The next phase is transforming TrainEfficiency into a true business operating system by identifying and surfacing revenue-generating opportunities automatically.

The goal is to answer:

“What actions can the coach take today to increase revenue?”

rather than:

“What statistics exist in the database?”

This is an audit phase.

Do not build new signals yet.

First identify every revenue opportunity signal that can already be generated using existing platform data.

Part 1 — Revenue Data Source Inventory

Audit every existing revenue-related table, service, workflow, and event source.

Inventory:

Lead Sources

* Lead submissions
* Landing page leads
* Team training leads
* Individual training leads
* Imported leads
* Lead capture forms
* Public lead routes

Sales Pipeline

* Deals
* Team training deals
* Opportunity stages
* Deal activity logs
* Follow-up tasks
* Revenue agent actions

Scheduling

* Assessments
* Consultations
* Discovery calls
* Evaluations
* Bookings
* Recurring bookings
* Coach schedules

Payments

* Invoices
* Stripe payments
* Failed payments

* Refunds

Subscriptions

* Expiring subscriptions

* Renewal events

Packages

* Session packages

Communications

* Email outreach
* SMS outreach
* Outreach drafts
* Outreach sequences

For each source provide:

* Table/service name
* Row count
* Organization scope
* Last activity timestamp
* Revenue relevance score (1–10)

Part 2 — Existing Revenue Signal Audit

Identify every signal already present that has direct revenue impact.

Examples:

* Revenue agent actions

* Current logic
* Accuracy assessment
* Business value score
* Production readiness score

Do not create them yet.

Part 4 — Revenue Opportunity Scoring

For every potential signal assign:

Revenue Impact Score (1–10)

Questions:

* Does it directly affect cash flow?
* Does it recover lost revenue?
* Does it increase conversion?
* Does it improve retention?
* Is there an immediate action available?

Classification:

Critical Revenue
High Revenue
Medium Revenue
Low Revenue
Noise
