## Brand & Style

The brand personality is authoritative, scientific, and relentlessly premium. It bridges the gap between the gritty world of athletic performance and the high-fidelity precision of modern SaaS (Apple/Stripe aesthetic). The target audience consists of coaches, parents, and serious youth athletes who value data-driven results over hype.

The design style is **Minimalist-Premium**. It leverages expansive white space, precise grid alignment, and high-contrast typography to create a sense of elite order. While the book cover features raw textures, the UI translates this into a "Software-First" execution: replacing grit with glassmorphism, smooth gradients, and refined tactile elements that suggest a sophisticated digital ecosystem.

## Colors

This design system utilizes a "Deep Tech" dark mode palette to evoke high-performance sports environments and premium hardware.

- **Primary (Gold):** Used exclusively for high-value actions, achievement states, and critical price points. It should feel like an "award" or a "gold standard" indicator.
- **Secondary (White/Off-White):** Primarily used for body text and prominent headings to ensure maximum legibility against the dark background.
- **Surface Tiers:** The background uses a true-black (`#000000`), while containers and cards use a tiered system of deep grays (`#0F0F0F`, `#1A1A1A`) to create depth without relying on traditional borders.
- **Accent/Success:** A subtle muted gold tint is used for soft shadows to maintain the warmth of the brand even in a digital-first interface.

## Typography

The typography strategy focuses on a hierarchy of "Performance Engineering."

- **Headlines:** Uses **Hanken Grotesk**. It is sharp, contemporary, and carries a "tech-forward" weight that commands attention. Tight letter-spacing for large displays mimics the impactful headers found on the book cover.
- **Body:** Uses **Inter**. This provides a neutral, systematic feel that ensures complex training data or book descriptions are easily digestible.
- **Accents/Data:** Uses **Space Grotesk**. This monospaced-influenced font is used for "meta-data" labels, technical specs, and call-outs to reinforce the scientific nature of the training system.

## Layout & Spacing

The layout adopts a **Fluid Grid** model with generous "Apple-style" breathing room. 

- **The 8px Rule:** All spacing between elements must be a multiple of 8px to ensure a rigid, mathematical harmony.
- **Rhythm:** Use large vertical gaps (120px+) between major sections to allow the user to focus on one value proposition at a time. 
- **Columns:** A 12-column grid is standard for desktop. For the book funnel, content should often be centered in a restricted 8-column container (approx. 800px) to maximize readability and focus.

## Elevation & Depth

Hierarchy is established through **Tonal Elevation** rather than stark shadows.

1.  **Base (Level 0):** Pure black background.
2.  **Raised (Level 1):** Subtle dark gray surfaces (`#0F0F0F`) for secondary content.
3.  **Interactive (Level 2):** Lighter gray cards (`#1A1A1A`) with "Soft Gold" shadows.
4.  **Shadow Character:** Shadows should be extremely diffused (32px-64px blur), low opacity (15-20%), and slightly tinted with the Primary Gold color to create a subtle glow effect around premium offerings.
5.  **Overlays:** Use backdrop blurs (20px+) for navigation bars and modal backdrops to maintain a sense of environmental continuity.

## Shapes

The shape language is "Sophisticated Geometric." 

- **Standard Radius:** All cards and primary containers use a 16px (`rounded-lg`) corner radius. 
- **Buttons:** Use higher roundedness (24px or full pill) to provide a soft, tactile contrast against the structured grid of the layout.
- **Iconography:** Icons should be thin-stroke (1.5px) and strictly linear, avoiding fills except for active "Gold" states.

## Components

- **Primary Action (CTA):** Large pill-shaped buttons with a Gold background and black text. On hover, apply a subtle inner-glow to simulate a physical light source.
- **Value Chips:** Use the `label-caps` typography inside small, outlined containers with 4px radii. These highlight "Evidence-Based" or "Limited Offer" tags.
- **Interactive Cards:** Features a 1px subtle border (`#333`) and a soft gold glow on hover. Content inside should be padded with at least 40px of internal spacing.
- **Input Fields:** Bottom-border only or very subtle filled containers. Focus state transitions the border color to Gold with a 2px stroke.
- **Pricing Toggles:** High-contrast switch components using the Stripe aesthetic—sliding pills that transition between "Physical Book" and "Digital Bundle" with smooth micro-animations.
- **Data Lists:** Use clean horizontal dividers with high transparency (10% white) and use the `Space Grotesk` font for numeric data points.
