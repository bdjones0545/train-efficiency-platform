Department OS v2 — Extraction Sprint

Goal:
Analyze Opportunity Acquisition and Hiring Department side-by-side, identify duplicated patterns, and extract them into reusable Department OS components.

This sprint is not about building Department #3.

This sprint is about reducing the cost of Departments #3–#10.

Success is measured by:

* Less duplicated code
* More reusable infrastructure
* Faster future department creation

The output should be:

Department OS v2
