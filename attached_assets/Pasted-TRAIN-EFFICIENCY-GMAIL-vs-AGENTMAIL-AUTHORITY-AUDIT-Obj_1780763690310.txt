TRAIN EFFICIENCY — GMAIL vs AGENTMAIL AUTHORITY AUDIT

Objective

Perform a complete audit of all communication systems across the TrainEfficiency codebase.

The purpose of this audit is to eliminate overlap, conflicting ownership, duplicate follow-ups, duplicate notifications, conflicting agents, and communication paths that bypass human approval.

This is an architecture audit only.

Do not modify code until the audit is complete.
