P2.1 IMPLEMENTATION — Revenue Opportunity Intelligence (Phase 1)

Objective

Implement the first Revenue Opportunity Intelligence signals inside the Attention Inbox.

These signals were identified in the P2 audit as:

* Highest revenue impact
* Highest actionability
* Lowest implementation effort
* Fully supported by existing schema

The goal is for every coach to open TrainEfficiency and immediately see the fastest path to generating revenue today.

Signals To Build

1. New Lead Submitted — Never Contacted
2. Lead Awaiting Follow-Up (72h+)
3. Free Intro / Assessment Completed — No Paid Conversion
4. Zero Upcoming Bookings This Week

These four signals become the foundation of:

TODAY’S REVENUE OPPORTUNITIES

Section.

Signal 1 — New Lead Submitted (Critical Revenue)

Purpose:

Detect leads that have entered the system and have never been contacted.

Criteria:

lead_capture_submissions

WHERE:

* contacted_at IS NULL
* status not converted
* status not lost
* created_at within last 7 days

Priority:

Critical

Attention Item:

Title:
“New lead awaiting first contact”

Body:
“[Lead Name] submitted [Offer Name] X hours ago and has not been contacted.”

Metadata:

* leadId
* leadName
* leadEmail
* leadPhone
* estimatedValue
* sourceOffer
* hoursWaiting

CTAs:

* Send Email
* Send SMS
* Schedule Call

Signal 2 — Lead Awaiting Follow-Up (72+ Hours)

Purpose:

Detect warm leads that have not received timely follow-up.

Criteria:

lead_capture_submissions

WHERE:

* contacted_at IS NOT NULL
* converted_at IS NULL
* booking_status != booked
* last_follow_up_at older than 72 hours

Priority:

Important

Attention Item:

Title:
“Lead awaiting follow-up”

Body:
“[Lead Name] has not been contacted in X days.”

Metadata:

* leadId
* followUpCount
* daysWaiting
* aiQualificationScore
* estimatedValue

CTAs:

* Send Follow-Up Email
* Send SMS
* Schedule Consultation

Signal 3 — Assessment Completed, No Paid Conversion

Purpose:

Capture the highest-intent conversion opportunity.

Criteria:

Bookings + Services

WHERE:

* Service categorized as:
    * Intro Session
    * Evaluation
    * Assessment
    * Consultation
* Session status = COMPLETED
* Completed more than 48 hours ago
* No paid booking exists afterward

Priority:

Critical

Attention Item:

Title:
“Assessment completed — no conversion”

Body:
“[Client Name] completed an assessment X days ago and has not purchased training.”

Metadata:

* clientId
* assessmentDate
* daysSinceAssessment
* estimatedAnnualValue

CTAs:

* Send Program Offer
* Schedule Follow-Up Call
* Generate Recommendation

Signal 4 — Zero Upcoming Bookings This Week

Purpose:

Surface empty schedule capacity.

Criteria:

Organization-level signal.

WHERE:

* No confirmed bookings in next 7 days

Priority:

Critical

Attention Item:

Title:
“No bookings scheduled this week”

Body:
“Your schedule has no confirmed bookings over the next 7 days.”

Metadata:

* openDays
* estimatedCapacity
* estimatedRevenueOpportunity

CTAs:

* Promote Availability
* Contact Leads
* Launch Follow-Up Campaign

Revenue Opportunity Section

Add:

TODAY’S REVENUE OPPORTUNITIES

Display order:

1. Critical
2. Important
3. Suggested

Sort by:

urgency DESC
business_impact DESC

Each item should show:

* Priority badge
* Estimated value
* Recommended action
* CTA buttons

Do not show generic counts.

Show specific opportunities.

Examples:

🔥 New lead submitted 47 minutes ago
Potential value: $297

⚠️ Assessment completed 3 days ago
Potential value: $840/year

💰 No bookings scheduled this week
Potential opportunity: $1,200

Deduplication Rules

Prevent duplicates:

* One active revenue item per lead
* One active conversion item per assessment
* One active schedule-capacity item per organization

The Attention Inbox should stop behaving like a monitoring dashboard and begin functioning like a revenue command center.

“What action can I take right now that could generate revenue?”
