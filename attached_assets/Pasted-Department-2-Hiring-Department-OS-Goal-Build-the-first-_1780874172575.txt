Department #2 — Hiring Department OS

Goal:
Build the first non-Opportunity department using Department OS v1.

This is not primarily a hiring feature.

This is a validation of the Department Framework.

Success is measured by how little custom infrastructure is required.

The Hiring Department should inherit:

* Department Lifecycle
* Department Events
* Department Health Checks
* Department Learning
* Executive Intelligence
* CEO Heartbeat Integration
* Department Registry

from Department OS v1.

⸻

MISSION

Create a Hiring Department capable of:

Candidate Discovery
→ Qualification
→ Outreach
→ Replies
→ Interview Pipeline
→ Hiring Outcomes
→ Learning
→ Executive Intelligence

The department should help organizations recruit:

* Coaches
* Trainers
* Interns
* Staff
* Contractors

Initially through manual candidate creation and internal workflow.

No external job scraping yet.

No automated hiring decisions.

VALIDATION GOAL

The primary objective is not hiring functionality.

The objective is proving that Department OS v1 can support a second department with minimal custom infrastructure.

If Hiring Department can be built substantially faster than Opportunity Acquisition, Department OS has been validated and TrainEfficiency officially becomes a multi-department AI operating system.
