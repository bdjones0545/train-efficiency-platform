Task 6 — Revenue Recognition + Coach Compensation Engine

Audit and build a proper revenue recognition and coach compensation system on top of the new accounting foundation.

Goal:
TrainEfficiency should correctly track:
- money collected
- revenue earned
- deferred revenue/liability
- coach compensation exposure
- payout calculations
- org-level profitability

while preserving immutable accounting and redemption history.

Context:
The platform now has:
- dollar accounting separation
- credit ledger events
- redemption lifecycle locks
- idempotent redemptions
- scheduling intelligence
- immutable redeemed sessions

Now build the financial recognition layer.

Core principle:
A payment is NOT automatically earned revenue.
Revenue is recognized when the service/session is delivered according to business rules.

Requirements:

1. Audit current payment/revenue flow
Search and document:
- wallet_transactions
- invoices
- payments
- subscriptions
- session purchases
- redemptions
- coach payouts
- commissions
- payroll-related calculations
- revenue dashboard calculations
- reporting endpoints

Identify:
- where money enters
- where revenue is currently considered “earned”
- where coach pay is calculated
- where deferred liability should exist but currently doesn’t

2. Create revenue recognition model
Establish these concepts explicitly:

Collected Revenue
Money received from client/org.

Deferred Revenue
Money owed in future services.

Recognized Revenue
Revenue earned after completed/redeemed session or fulfilled service.

Coach Compensation Exposure
What the coach has earned but may not yet be paid.

Net Org Revenue
Recognized revenue minus compensation obligations.

Do NOT collapse these into one metric.

3. Revenue ledger events
Create immutable ledger event support for:
- payment_received
- revenue_recognized
- deferred_revenue_created
- deferred_revenue_released
- coach_compensation_accrued
- coach_compensation_paid
- refund_issued
- cancellation_reversal
- manual_adjustment

Each should include:
- orgId
- userId/clientId
- coachId if applicable
- bookingId/sessionId if applicable
- amountCents
- reason
- source action
- createdBy
- timestamps

4. Revenue recognition trigger rules
Define and enforce:

Package/session purchase:
- increases collected revenue
- increases deferred revenue liability
- does NOT immediately recognize revenue

Completed + redeemed session:
- decreases deferred revenue
- recognizes earned revenue
- accrues coach compensation

Cancelled unredeemed session:
- no recognition

Cancelled redeemed session:
- reversal event if current rules allow

Unlimited memberships:
- configurable recognition model
- monthly recognition or attendance-based if supported

Team contracts:
- support org-level recurring recognition

5. Coach compensation engine
Build configurable coach payout rules.

Support:
- flat per-session
- percentage split
- per-service rates
- org-level overrides
- future commission support

Examples:
- Coach earns $40 per session
- Coach earns 60% of recognized revenue
- Team training pays flat $100/session
- Different services pay different rates

6. Compensation accruals
Coach earnings should accrue automatically when:
- session becomes completed + redeemed
- or another recognized fulfillment rule occurs

Do NOT accrue on:
- scheduled only
- cancelled
- no-show unless explicitly configured

7. Payout tracking
Separate:
- accrued earnings
- paid earnings
- pending payouts

Support:
- payout periods
- payroll exports later
- manual payout marking
- audit trail of payout events

8. Dashboard/reporting updates
Audit dashboards and analytics.

Add or separate:
- collected revenue
- recognized revenue
- deferred liability
- pending coach payouts
- paid coach payouts
- projected revenue
- average revenue per session
- revenue by service
- revenue by coach
- org profitability

Do NOT display inflated revenue by counting unfulfilled packages as earned revenue.

9. Coach-facing visibility
Coach dashboards should show:
- completed sessions
- accrued earnings
- pending payout
- paid payout history
- payout breakdown by service/session

10. Agent alignment
Scheduling/payment agents must understand:
- scheduling does not equal earned revenue
- completed redeemed sessions trigger recognition
- cancellation may reverse accruals
- payout previews should be informational only unless admin-confirmed

11. Integrity protections
Ensure:
- revenue cannot recognize twice for same redemption
- coach compensation cannot accrue twice
- deleting sessions cannot erase recognized history
- completed redeemed sessions remain immutable
- reversal events are additive, not destructive

12. Diagnostic endpoint
Create read-only admin integrity diagnostics:
GET /api/admin/revenue-integrity

Checks:
- duplicate revenue recognition
- duplicate coach accruals
- negative deferred revenue
- recognized revenue with no fulfillment event
- coach payout mismatch
- sessions recognized without redemption
- orphaned payout records

13. Testing
Add regression tests for:
- package purchase creates deferred revenue
- completed redeemed session recognizes revenue once
- coach accrual generated once
- duplicate redemption does not double-recognize revenue
- cancellation reversal logic
- payout totals correct
- org profitability calculations
- percentage split calculations
- flat-rate calculations
- negative deferred revenue prevention

Deliverables:
- Files changed
- Revenue model summary
- Compensation model summary
- Ledger event architecture
- Dashboard/reporting changes
- Integrity protections added
- Diagnostic endpoint summary
- Tests added
- Confirmation that Task 2 lifecycle locks still pass
