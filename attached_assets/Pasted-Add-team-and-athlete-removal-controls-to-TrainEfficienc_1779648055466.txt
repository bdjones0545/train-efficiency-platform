Add team and athlete removal controls to TrainEfficiency.

Goal:
Org admins and authorized team coaches should be able to remove teams and remove athletes from teams safely.

Permissions:
- Org owner/admin: can delete/archive any team in the org and remove any athlete from any team.
- Team coach/creator: can delete/archive teams they own/manage and remove athletes from their teams.
- Staff without team ownership: view only unless explicitly assigned.
- Athletes/clients: no delete access.

Important:
Prefer soft-delete/archive over hard delete.

Team deletion:
1. Add backend endpoint:
   DELETE /api/org/coach/teams/:teamId

Behavior:
- Validate org session with resolveOrgSession()
- Verify user is org admin/owner OR team coach/creator
- Soft delete team:
  - set archivedAt/deletedAt
  - set status = "archived" or "deleted"
- Do not destroy historical PR/workout/activity data.
- Hide archived/deleted teams from active team lists.

2. Add frontend delete/archive action:
- On team detail page
- Optional overflow menu on team card
- Confirmation dialog requiring team name confirmation
- Warning: athletes and historical records will remain, but the team will be removed from active views.

Athlete removal:
1. Add backend endpoint:
   DELETE /api/org/coach/teams/:teamId/athletes/:userId

Behavior:
- Validate org session
- Verify admin/owner OR team coach/creator
- Remove athlete from pr_team_members or mark membership inactive
- Do not delete the user account
- Do not delete athlete PRs, workouts, education, or messages
- Log activity event: athlete_removed_from_team

2. Add frontend remove action:
- On team roster row/card
- Confirmation dialog
- Update React Query caches immediately after success

Optional org-level athlete archive:
- Add separate action later for org admins only:
  Archive athlete from org
- This should be different from “remove from team.”

Activity logging:
Log events for:
- team_archived
- athlete_removed_from_team

Invalidate:
- teams list
- team detail
- athlete detail
- org activity feed
- command center

Acceptance tests:
- Org admin can archive any team.
- Team coach can archive their own team.
- Team coach cannot archive another coach’s team.
- Athlete can never see delete controls.
- Removing athlete from team hides them from roster but keeps their profile/history.
- No hard deletion of historical records.
