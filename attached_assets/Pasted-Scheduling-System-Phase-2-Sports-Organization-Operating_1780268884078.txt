2. Athlete Compatibility Engine

Before displaying Join Session:

Compare:

* Athlete Age
* Athlete Sport
* Athlete Training Level

Against session requirements.

Display:

🟢 Recommended

🟡 Eligible

🔴 Not Recommended

Examples:

Recommended:
Athlete matches age, sport, and skill level.

Eligible:
Athlete fits age but different sport.

Not Recommended:
Athlete outside age range or skill requirements.

Do NOT block registration initially.

Only provide guidance.

Future versions may enforce restrictions.

7. Waitlist Automation

Current waitlist system exists.

Enhance it.

When a registered athlete cancels:

1. Find first athlete in waitlist order.
2. Create reservation hold.
3. Send notification.
4. Hold expires after configurable period (default 2 hours).
5. If not accepted, move to next athlete.

Track:

* Waitlist Position
* Promotion Status
* Promotion History
