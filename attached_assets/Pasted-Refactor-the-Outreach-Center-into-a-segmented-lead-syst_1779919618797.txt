Refactor the Outreach Center into a segmented lead system.

Problem:
The Outreach Center currently mixes:
- individual athlete leads
- team training leads
- organization prospects
- outreach contacts

with no visual distinction or filtering.

Example:
Taylah Page (individual athlete lead from Summer Speed Sprint) appears in the same prospect list as Egret & Iron Golf (organization/team prospect).

Requirements:

1. Add Lead Type System Everywhere

Standardize:
- individual_athlete_lead
- team_training_lead
- organization_prospect
- outreach_contact

Expose leadType in:
- API responses
- DB queries
- UI cards
- filters
- badges
- analytics

2. Outreach Center UI Refactor

At the top of Outreach Center add segmented tabs:

- All
- Athlete Leads
- Team Training
- Organizations
- Outreach Contacts

Tabs should filter instantly client-side.

3. Add Lead Type Badge To Every Card

Examples:

Athlete lead:
Badge:
🏃 Athlete Lead

Team training:
Badge:
🏆 Team Training

Organization:
Badge:
🏢 Organization

Outreach contact:
Badge:
✉️ Outreach Contact

Color-code them.

4. Add Filters

Add filters for:
- Lead Type
- Pipeline Stage
- Temperature
- Campaign
- Sport
- City
- Status
- Requires Approval
- Has Active Gmail Draft
- Scheduling Status

5. Athlete Lead Card Layout

Athlete cards should show:
- athlete name
- parent
- age/grade
- school
- sport
- campaign source
- lead score
- temperature
- suggested next action
- pipeline stage
- Gmail draft status

6. Team Training Card Layout

Show:
- organization/team name
- category
- city/state
- decision maker
- outreach status
- deal value
- stage
- contact confidence
- outreach history

7. Add “Source” Visibility

Each card should show:
- Lead Capture Form
- Deep Search
- Manual Entry
- Referral
- Meta Ad
- Gmail Reply Recovery
- Import

8. Query Separation

Team Training queries must exclude:
leadType = individual_athlete_lead

Athlete Lead queries must include:
leadType = individual_athlete_lead

9. Backfill Existing Data

Taylah Page:
leadType = individual_athlete_lead

Egret & Iron Golf:
leadType = organization_prospect or team_training_lead

10. Add Stats Bar

Top metrics:
- Athlete Leads
- Team Prospects
- Hot Leads
- Requires Approval
- Scheduling
- Booked
- Outreach Pending

11. Add Smart Empty States

If viewing Athlete Leads:
show:
“Individual athlete leads from campaigns and landing pages appear here.”

If viewing Team Training:
show:
“Schools, clubs, organizations, and group opportunities appear here.”

12. Mobile UX

Current mobile layout is crowded.

Improve:
- collapsible metadata
- compact badges
- expandable details
- sticky filters
- segmented control at top
- quick actions row
