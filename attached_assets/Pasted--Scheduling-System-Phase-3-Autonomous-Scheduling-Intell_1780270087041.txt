# Scheduling System Phase 3 — Autonomous Scheduling Intelligence

Phase 2 is complete and operational.

Do not remove, regress, or redesign any existing functionality.

Build Phase 3 as an intelligence and optimization layer that sits on top of the existing scheduling infrastructure.

The goal is to transform scheduling from a calendar and registration system into an autonomous business optimization engine.

---

## 1. Revenue Recovery Engine

Create a scheduling intelligence service that continuously analyzes session performance.

Identify:

- Sessions below 50% capacity
- Sessions occurring within the next 72 hours
- Sessions with declining registration trends
- Sessions generating less revenue than expected

Calculate:

- Revenue Gap
- Capacity Gap
- Potential Revenue Recovery

Example:

High School Football Speed

Capacity: 8
Registered: 3
Revenue: $90
Potential Revenue: $240
Revenue Gap: $150

Generate recommendations:

- Contact previous attendees
- Send reminder campaign
- Promote on social media
- Merge with another session
- Adjust schedule

Display recommendations inside the Scheduling Command Center.

## Success Criteria

The scheduling system should evolve from a management tool into an autonomous scheduling intelligence platform that:

- Identifies revenue opportunities
- Forecasts demand
- Detects retention risks
- Optimizes coach utilization
- Recommends new sessions
- Recommends schedule changes
- Creates outreach campaigns
- Prioritizes opportunities
- Provides executive-level scheduling intelligence

while maintaining full multi-tenant isolation, role-based permissions, mobile responsiveness, and compatibility with the existing Workflow Engine, Agent Infrastructure, Revenue Intelligence, and TrainChat ecosystem.
