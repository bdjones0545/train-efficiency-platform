PHASE 20 — ORGANIZATIONAL PERFORMANCE & STRATEGIC ALIGNMENT SYSTEM

Objective

Create a company-wide strategic execution framework that aligns departments, digital employees, objectives, decisions, tasks, SOPs, and initiatives around measurable organizational outcomes.

Core Principle

Organization answers:

Who owns the work?

Strategy answers:

Why the work exists.

The purpose of Phase 20 is to connect every action performed by the workforce to company objectives.
