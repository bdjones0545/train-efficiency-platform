Audit the Team Training Leads system architecture and separate B2B partnership leads from consumer athlete intake leads.

Problem:
Individual athlete leads from Meta ads and landing pages are currently being rendered inside the Team Training Leads outreach engine.

This creates:
- incorrect actions (Deep Search, Generate Email, Likely Valid)
- distorted pipeline metrics
- confusing UX
- mixed lead semantics
- B2B and B2C workflows collapsed together

Required architecture split:

SECTION 1 — Team Training Partnerships (B2B)
Purpose:
Research and outreach to schools, clubs, facilities, and organizations.

Allowed actions:
- Deep Search
- Generate Outreach Email
- Create Deal
- Mark Replied
- Do Not Contact
- Pipeline valuation
- Organization enrichment

Entity types:
- school
- club
- facility
- academy
- director
- coach organization

SECTION 2 — Athlete Leads / Intake Pipeline (B2C)
Purpose:
Manage athlete applications and parent inquiries from ads, forms, and landing pages.

Replace current cards with athlete-focused CRM cards.

Card actions:
- Review Application
- Contact Parent
- Schedule Evaluation
- Send Intake Form
- Convert to Athlete
- Assign Program
- Archive Lead

Card metadata:
- athlete name
- parent
- sport
- school
- age
- readiness/commitment
- source campaign
- submitted timestamp
- application status

Remove completely from athlete cards:
- Deep Search
- Likely Valid
- General Email
- organization enrichment
- outreach confidence bars

Metrics for athlete pipeline:
- total applications
- evaluations scheduled
- conversion rate
- enrolled athletes
- projected revenue
- source attribution

Technical requirements:
1. Add explicit leadType:
   - team_partnership
   - athlete_intake
2. Migrate existing athlete ad leads automatically.
3. Create separate tabs/pages:
   - Team Partnerships
   - Athlete Leads
4. Preserve shared underlying CRM/deal infrastructure where possible.
5. Keep EST dark styling but use different visual language:
   - B2B = intelligence/prospecting
   - Athlete Leads = onboarding/performance pipeline
6. Mobile-first optimized cards.

Acceptance criteria:
- Athlete leads never appear inside organization prospecting workflows.
- B2B outreach tools only appear for partnership leads.
- Pipeline metrics accurately reflect their category.
- UX clearly communicates two distinct business systems.
