Perform a complete architectural and systems audit of the AI Operations layer inside TrainEfficiency.

The goal is to evolve AI Operations from a collection of automation tools into a true organizational AI infrastructure system where connected APIs and AI agents function like operational employees/departments inside a company.

* Attention Inbox
* Business Brain
* Agent Ops Monitor
* Workflow Orchestrator
* Workflows
* Trigger Audit
* Agent Tools

* What the section currently represents conceptually
* What operational/business problem it solves
* What role it plays inside the organization

* Likely current implementation patterns
* Strengths
* Weaknesses
* Scalability concerns
* UX concerns
