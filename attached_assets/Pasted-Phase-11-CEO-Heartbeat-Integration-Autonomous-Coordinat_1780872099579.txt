Phase 11 — CEO Heartbeat Integration & Autonomous Coordination

Goal:
Promote Opportunity Acquisition from a standalone operating system into a fully integrated organizational department inside TrainEfficiency.

Current State:

Opportunity Acquisition operates independently.

Discovery
→ Qualification
→ Outreach
→ Execution
→ Replies
→ Learning
→ Executive Intelligence

New State:

CEO Heartbeat
↓
Opportunity Executive Coordinator
↓
Opportunity Acquisition OS
↓
Attention Inbox
↓
Executive Brief
↓
Best Action Today

Opportunity Acquisition should become a continuous organizational capability rather than a page users must manually visit.

Important:

Do NOT enable autonomous outreach.

Do NOT enable autonomous negotiation.

Do NOT enable autonomous pricing.

Do NOT enable autonomous meeting scheduling.

CEO Heartbeat should coordinate and prioritize, not execute external communications.

SUCCESS CRITERIA

Phase 11 is complete when:

* Opportunity Executive Coordinator exists
* CEO Heartbeat can review Opportunity Acquisition health
* Attention Inbox receives opportunity alerts
* Best Action Today can include opportunity actions
* Opportunity dashboard card exists
* Heartbeat summary endpoint exists
* Executive brief aggregation exists
* Agent events are logged
* No autonomous external actions occur
* Zero TypeScript errors

Result:

Opportunity Acquisition graduates from an isolated subsystem into a fully integrated department within the TrainEfficiency operating system, continuously contributing intelligence, alerts, and recommendations to organizational leadership.
