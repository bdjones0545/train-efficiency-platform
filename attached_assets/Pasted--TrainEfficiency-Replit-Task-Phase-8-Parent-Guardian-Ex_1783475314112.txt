# Goal

TrainEfficiency serves many youth athletes.

Parents are often:

* Paying customers
* Decision makers
* Scheduling coordinators
* Primary communication contacts

The system should treat parents as first-class participants in the onboarding experience without building a full parent portal.

This phase focuses on communication, visibility, and relationship management.

---

# Core Philosophy

Parents are **contacts**, not athletes.

Do not duplicate athlete data.

Everything should relate back to the athlete while preserving the parent relationship.
