Decision Journal Auto-Capture Audit & Implementation

Goal:
Apply the same philosophy used for Hermes Learnings.

The Decision Journal should primarily be populated automatically from real agent and human decisions occurring throughout the platform.

The manual decision form should remain available, but it should not be the primary source of decision history.

Current Problem:
The Decisions tab is form-first.
