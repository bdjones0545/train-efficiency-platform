Redesign the floating AI assistant (green floating action button) to become the CEO Agent / Executive Agent for the entire AI workforce.

Current issue:
The floating assistant currently feels like a Revenue Agent dashboard with a chat interface attached.

It exposes:
- Revenue chat
- Revenue metrics
- Revenue settings
- Revenue actions

This creates the impression that the assistant is a single-purpose revenue bot.

The floating assistant should represent the CEO layer, not the Revenue department.
