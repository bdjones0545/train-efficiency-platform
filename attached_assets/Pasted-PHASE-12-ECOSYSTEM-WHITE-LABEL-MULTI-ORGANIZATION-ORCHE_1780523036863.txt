PHASE 12 — ECOSYSTEM, WHITE-LABEL & MULTI-ORGANIZATION ORCHESTRATION

Objective

Transform TrainEfficiency from an AI-powered business operating system into a platform ecosystem capable of supporting franchises, agencies, consultants, enterprise groups, school districts, sports organizations, and large multi-location businesses.

Core Principle

Phases 1–11:
Operate one organization.

Phase 12:
Operate thousands of organizations.

This phase introduces ecosystem-level management, white-label deployment, enterprise hierarchies, template distribution, and platform administration.
