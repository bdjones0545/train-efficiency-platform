PHASE 4 — EXECUTIVE INTELLIGENCE SYSTEM

Objective

Create an Executive Intelligence layer above AI Operations that functions as the organization’s AI CEO advisor.

The Executive Intelligence system should not report activity.

It should interpret activity.

Core Principle

AI Operations answers:

“What happened?”

Executive Intelligence answers:

“What should I do next?”
