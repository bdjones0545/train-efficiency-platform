AI Workforce Management Dashboard — Phase 2 Upgrade

Objective

Transform the AI Workforce Management Dashboard from a configuration screen into a true executive operating center that provides visibility, governance, performance monitoring, workforce optimization, and agent deployment management.

1. Workforce Health Score

Add a prominent Workforce Health card at the top of the page.

Metrics:

* Workforce Health Score (0–100)
* Agents Online / Total Agents
* Integrations Connected / Available Integrations
* Governance Coverage %
* Workflow Coverage %
* Active Departments %

Scoring Factors:

* Agent uptime
* Connected integrations
* Governance completeness
* Workflow enablement
* Approval configuration

* Red: Below 70
