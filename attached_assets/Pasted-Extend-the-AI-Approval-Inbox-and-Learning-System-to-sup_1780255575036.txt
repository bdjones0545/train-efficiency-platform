Extend the AI Approval Inbox and Learning System to support Team Training Leads, School Partnerships, Organization Outreach, and Employment Opportunities.

Current State:
The approval workflow, feedback loop, learning rules, and autonomy progression currently focus on athlete/parent lead communications.

Goal:
Create one unified communication learning architecture that works across all outbound communication types.

⸻

Part 1 — Communication Domains

Add:

communication_domain

Values:

* athlete_lead
* parent_lead
* team_training
* school_partnership
* athletic_director
* coach_outreach
* organization_outreach
* business_outreach
* employment_opportunity
* corporate_wellness
* facility_partnership

All approvals, feedback, learning rules, revisions, and autonomy settings must include communication_domain.

⸻

Part 2 — AI Communications Center

Rename:

AI Approvals

To:

AI Communications Center

Add tabs:

* Athlete Leads
* Team Training
* Schools
* Organizations
* Employment
* All Communications

Each tab should display:

* Drafts
* Pending Approvals
* Sent Messages
* Replies
* Rejections
* Autonomy Status

⸻

Part 3 — Team Training Lead Learning

Support:

* Initial outreach
* Follow-up
* Discovery meeting request
* Proposal follow-up
* Objection handling
* Re-engagement

Allow:

* Approve
* Reject
* Edit
* Regenerate with feedback
* Coaching comments

Use the same learning loop already built for athlete leads.

⸻

Part 4 — Organization Outreach Learning

Support:

* Gym owners
* Facility owners
* Sports organizations
* Universities
* Recreation departments
* Corporate wellness

Track:

* Role
* Organization type
* Industry
* Lead source

Store:

* Preferred CTA
* Preferred pitch style
* Preferred message length
* Common objections
* Successful talking points

⸻

Part 5 — Outcome Tracking

For all communication domains:

Track:

* Opened
* Replied
* Meeting booked
* Proposal requested
* Proposal accepted
* Contract signed
* Converted
* Lost

Store outcome data and connect it to learning rules.

⸻

Part 6 — Autonomy Progression By Domain

Autonomy should be calculated independently for:

* Athlete Leads
* Team Training
* School Partnerships
* Organization Outreach
* Employment

Example:

Athlete Leads = Level 3
Team Training = Level 1
School Partnerships = Level 0

Do not use a global autonomy score.

Acceptance Criteria

1. Team training messages can be reviewed exactly like athlete lead messages.
2. Organization outreach messages can be reviewed exactly like athlete lead messages.
3. Every communication domain has its own learning rules.
4. Every communication domain has its own autonomy score.
5. Every communication domain tracks outcomes.
6. The AI learns separately how to talk to parents, athletic directors, coaches, and business owners.
7. Existing athlete-lead learning continues to function unchanged.
8. Mobile UI remains fully usable.
9. Return files changed, migrations created, routes added, screenshots, and migrated record counts.
