Phase 6 — Autonomy Progression

Every domain starts at:

Level 0
Manual Approval Required

No auto-send initially.

Promotion requires:

* 20+ reviewed examples
* 90% approval rate
* low repeated mistakes
* high rule compliance
* admin approval

Autonomy must remain domain-specific.

Example:

Athlete Leads = Level 3

Team Training = Level 1

Athletic Director = Level 0

Employment = Level 0
