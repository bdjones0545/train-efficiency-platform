Build AgentMail Follow-Up Sequencing for TrainEfficiency.

Goal:
After inbound emails and approved/sent replies, TrainEfficiency should create supervised follow-up sequences so leads, applicants, scheduling requests, support issues, and partner inquiries do not go cold.

Human approval must remain mandatory before any outbound email is sent.

2. Sequence rules

Create default follow-up templates by classification:

new_lead / pricing_question:
- Step 1: 24 hours after first reply
- Step 2: 3 days after first reply
- Step 3: 7 days after first reply

booking_request / reschedule_request:
- Step 1: 12 hours after first reply if no booking confirmed
- Step 2: 24 hours after first reply

employment_candidate:
- Step 1: 24 hours after reply
- Step 2: 4 days after reply

coach_partner_inquiry:
- Step 1: 24 hours after reply
- Step 2: 5 days after reply

support_issue / billing_issue / software_bug_report:
- Step 1: 24 hours after support reply
- Step 2: 72 hours after support reply

general_question:
- Step 1: 3 days after reply

4. Stop conditions

Cancel or skip follow-ups when:

- recipient replies
- booking is completed
- lead converted
- applicant moved to next stage
- support issue resolved
- admin manually cancels
- email bounced/failed
- unsubscribe or stop language detected

Important:
Do not send follow-ups automatically.
When a follow-up becomes due, create a pending approval item in the Follow-Up Queue.

5. Approval workflow

Add routes:

GET /api/agentmail/followups
GET /api/agentmail/followups/:id
PATCH /api/agentmail/followups/:id
POST /api/agentmail/followups/:id/approve
POST /api/agentmail/followups/:id/reject
POST /api/agentmail/followups/:id/send
POST /api/agentmail/followups/:id/cancel
POST /api/agentmail/followups/process-due

Rules:
- send requires approval_status = approved
- edited body should be preserved
- duplicate send protection required
- org scoped and permission protected

6. Scheduling

Use the existing Workflow Job Queue or cron pattern.

Every 15–30 minutes:
- find scheduled follow-ups due now
- check stop conditions
- if valid, move to pending_review
- create Attention Inbox item: “Follow-up awaiting approval”
