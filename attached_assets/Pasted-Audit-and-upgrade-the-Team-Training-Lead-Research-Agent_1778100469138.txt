Audit and upgrade the Team Training Lead Research Agent so generated leads prioritize decision-maker contact info.

Current problem:
The lead generator is finding organizations, but many leads have “No Email.” For outreach, the email should ideally belong to a decision maker, not just the business in general.

Goal:
Every lead should attempt to include a decision-maker contact or be clearly marked as incomplete.

Required changes:

1. Lead research prompt / agent logic
Update the research instructions so the agent searches for:
- owner
- director
- athletic director
- head coach
- program director
- team director
- operations manager
- training coordinator
- general manager

For each lead, prioritize emails in this order:
1. named decision-maker email
2. role-based decision-maker email, such as director@, coach@, athletics@, teams@, info@ if clearly connected to team/program decision-making
3. general organization email
4. no email found

2. Add decision-maker fields to saved leads
Add or support these fields:
- decisionMakerName
- decisionMakerTitle
- decisionMakerEmail
- contactConfidence
- contactSourceUrl
- contactQuality

contactQuality should be one of:
- decision_maker
- role_based
- general
- missing

3. Scoring update
A lead with no email should not receive an 80%+ score unless it has very strong other signals.
Update scoring:
- decision-maker email: strong positive signal
- role-based email: moderate positive signal
- general email: small positive signal
- missing email: strong penalty

4. UI update
Replace the current “No Email” badge with clearer contact status:
- “Decision Maker”
- “Role Email”
- “General Email”
- “Needs Contact”

For “Needs Contact,” show a secondary action:
- “Find Contact”
This should trigger a contact enrichment attempt for that specific lead.

5. Email generation guardrail
Disable or warn on “Generate Email” when no usable email exists.
Instead of generating an email immediately, show:
“Contact needed before outreach.”
Button should become:
“Find Decision Maker”

6. Contact enrichment route
Add an endpoint/action to enrich one existing lead:
POST /api/team-training-leads/:id/enrich-contact

This should search for a decision-maker contact for the saved organization and update the lead if found.

7. Agent transparency
When expanded, the lead card should show:
- contact type
- decision maker name/title if available
- source URL
- confidence
- “No verified contact found yet” if missing

Acceptance criteria:
- New leads strongly prefer decision-maker emails.
- Leads with no email are marked “Needs Contact,” not treated like ready outreach leads.
- “Generate Email” is blocked or redirected to contact enrichment when no email exists.
- Lead score drops when email is missing.
- Individual leads can be enriched after creation.
- No TypeScript errors.
