PHASE 11 — AUTONOMOUS BUSINESS EXECUTION ENGINE

Objective

Transform TrainEfficiency from a business intelligence platform into a business execution platform capable of deploying, managing, measuring, and optimizing real-world business initiatives.

Core Principle

Layers 1–10:
Understand the business.

Phase 11:
Change the business.

The platform should no longer stop at recommendations.

It should execute approved strategies.
