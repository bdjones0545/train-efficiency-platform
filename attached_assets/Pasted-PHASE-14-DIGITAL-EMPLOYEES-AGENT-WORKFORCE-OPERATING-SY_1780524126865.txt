PHASE 14 — DIGITAL EMPLOYEES & AGENT WORKFORCE OPERATING SYSTEM

Objective

Transform AI agents from workflow tools into persistent digital employees with roles, responsibilities, performance reviews, goals, compensation models, career paths, and organizational structures.

Core Principle

Current State:

Agents perform tasks.

Phase 14:

Agents become workforce members.

Organizations should manage AI employees exactly like human employees.
