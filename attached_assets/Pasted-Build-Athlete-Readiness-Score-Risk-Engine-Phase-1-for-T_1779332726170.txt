Build Athlete Readiness Score + Risk Engine Phase 1 for TrainEfficiency.

GOAL:
Create a centralized athlete status and intervention engine that continuously evaluates athlete readiness, consistency, fatigue, education compliance, and workout behavior.

This becomes the intelligence layer connecting:
- Workout Builder
- Readiness Check-ins
- PR Tracker
- Education
- Notifications
- Timeline
- AI Recommendations

====================================================
1. CORE CONCEPT
====================================================

Every athlete receives a continuously updated:

Athlete Status Score
0–100

And a:

Risk Level
- Green
- Yellow
- Red

The system should synthesize:
- readiness
- fatigue
- soreness
- missed workouts
- workout adherence
- PR stagnation
- education compliance
- hydration/recovery education completion
- quiz failures
- inactivity streaks

====================================================
3. STATUS SCORE ENGINE
====================================================

Generate composite scores:

A. Readiness Score
Based on:
- readiness check-ins
- fatigue
- soreness
- sleep
- stress
- motivation

B. Adherence Score
Based on:
- workout completion %
- missed sessions
- consistency streaks
- late completions

C. Recovery Score
Based on:
- soreness trends
- fatigue trends
- hydration education completion
- recovery education completion

D. Education Score
Based on:
- pathway completion
- quiz scores
- overdue modules
- failed quizzes

E. Engagement Score
Based on:
- portal usage
- recent activity
- streaks
- education participation

Final:
0–100 weighted composite.

====================================================
4. RISK CLASSIFICATION
====================================================

Green
- healthy
- compliant
- engaged

Yellow
- moderate concern
- declining readiness
- missed sessions
- incomplete recovery education

Red
- high fatigue
- repeated low readiness
- multiple missed workouts
- low engagement
- severe soreness trend

====================================================
5. RULE ENGINE
====================================================

Generate flags automatically.

Examples:

IF:
readiness <= 4 three times in 7 days
THEN:
create low_readiness flag

IF:
2+ missed workouts in 14 days
THEN:
missed_workouts flag

IF:
soreness >= 8 repeatedly
THEN:
recovery_risk flag

IF:
hydration education incomplete
AND fatigue high
THEN:
hydration_risk flag

IF:
PR stagnant > 45 days
AND adherence high
THEN:
coach_review recommendation

====================================================
6. AI RECOMMENDATION ENGINE
====================================================

Create AI recommendation endpoint:

POST /api/org/athlete-intelligence/recommend

AI receives:
- readiness history
- soreness trends
- workout adherence
- PR history
- education completion
- coach notes
- recent activity

AI suggests:
- recovery education
- hydration pathway
- reduced workload
- coach review
- deload recommendation
- motivation intervention

IMPORTANT:
AI NEVER auto-applies changes.
Coach approval required.
