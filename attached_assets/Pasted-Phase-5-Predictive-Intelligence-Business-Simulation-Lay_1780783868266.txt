Phase 5 — Predictive Intelligence & Business Simulation Layer

Objective

Move TrainEfficiency from reacting to the business to predicting the future state of the business.

The platform should answer:

* What is likely to happen?
* What risks are emerging?
* What opportunities are forming?
* What action today creates the best future outcome?

⸻

1. Organizational Forecast Engine

Create a forecasting service that projects:

30 Days
60 Days
90 Days
180 Days

Predictions:

* Revenue
* Lead volume
* Conversion rate
* Retention rate
* Capacity utilization
* Coach staffing needs
* Session demand

Every forecast should include:

* Confidence score
* Supporting factors
* Historical basis
* Variance range

⸻

2. CEO Future State Dashboard

New page:

/admin/forecast

Sections:

Revenue Forecast
Growth Forecast
Retention Forecast
Capacity Forecast
Risk Forecast

Display:

Current State
Projected State
Change %
Confidence

⸻

3. Business Risk Detection Engine

Continuously monitor:

* Lead decline
* Attendance decline
* Coach utilization drops
* Revenue slowdown
* Retention deterioration
* Pipeline weakness

Generate:

Low Risk
Medium Risk
High Risk
Critical Risk

before problems occur.

⸻

4. Opportunity Detection Engine

Continuously identify:

* Expansion opportunities
* Hiring opportunities
* Pricing opportunities
* Capacity opportunities
* Marketing opportunities

Example:

“Girls performance training demand increased 28% over trailing 60 days.”

“Recommend opening 2 additional sessions.”

Lead impact
Utilization impact
Profit impact
Risk impact

Goal

Transform TrainEfficiency from:

System of Record
→ System of Learning
→ System of Trust
→ System of Autonomy
→ System of Prediction
