Create:
AI Workforce Onboarding Wizard

Goal:
Allow new organizations to “hire and configure” their AI staff in a guided onboarding flow.

Route:

* /onboarding/ai-workforce

Flow:

1. Welcome screen

* explain AI workforce concept
* explain governance/safety
* show visual AI org preview

2. Choose organizational goals
    Examples:

* improve retention
* automate scheduling
* reduce admin work

    Examples:

    Pre-selected starter workflows based on goals.

* AI workforce summary

PART 2 — Simplified vs Advanced Workspace Modes

Expand workspace modes globally.

Modes:

1. Simplified

* hides technical complexity
* hides governance internals
* uses natural language explanations
* focuses on outcomes and recommendations

2. Advanced

* full operational visibility
* workflow topology
* governance analytics
* execution graphs
* queue health
* audit systems

Operators can switch at any time.

Persist preference per user.
