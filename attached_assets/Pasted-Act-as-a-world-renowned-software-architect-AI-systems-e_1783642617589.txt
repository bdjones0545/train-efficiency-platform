# Page

**Opportunity Inbox**

This page should be the centralized work queue for business opportunities, operational risks, revenue recovery, lead follow-up, scheduling opportunities, client success actions, retention risks, and AI-recommended next steps.

This page is not just an inbox.

It is the place where TrainEfficiency turns signals into action.

Do NOT redesign the page.

Do NOT remove existing functionality.

Do NOT create fake AI.

Do NOT add placeholder buttons.

Do NOT invent metrics.

Preserve the existing architecture, backend intelligence, and TrainEfficiency neon green/black identity.

Improve the experience, functionality, explainability, agent utilization, and closed-loop learning.

# Core Mission

Opportunity Inbox should answer:

What opportunities exist right now?

Which one matters most?

Why does it matter?

Which agent found it?

What is the expected business impact?

What should I do next?

Can I approve, reject, dismiss, or delegate it?

Did the action work?

Did the system learn from the outcome?

The page should reduce decision fatigue and convert intelligence into measurable action.
