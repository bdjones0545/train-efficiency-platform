TRAIN EFFICIENCY — MOBILE POLISH & UX REFINEMENT SPRINT

Objective:

Stop building major features.

Shift into product refinement mode.

Review all existing pages and improve usability, consistency, mobile experience, information hierarchy, navigation clarity, visual polish, and conversion flow.

The goal is to make TrainEfficiency feel like a finished professional SaaS platform rather than a collection of powerful features.

==================================================
PHASE 1 — MOBILE UX AUDIT

Perform a complete mobile-first audit of every page.

Requirements:

* No horizontal scrolling anywhere
* No clipped text
* No truncated buttons
* No inaccessible controls
* No floating button overlap
* No modal overflow issues
* No hidden actions below viewport
* All pages optimized for 375px width

Verify:

Scheduling
Availability
Open Group Sessions
My Bookings
Athlete Leads
Team Training Leads
Outreach Center
Lead Pipeline
Branding
CEO Heartbeat
Command Center
Notifications
Settings
Athlete Profiles
Team Profiles

Fix any remaining mobile responsiveness issues.
