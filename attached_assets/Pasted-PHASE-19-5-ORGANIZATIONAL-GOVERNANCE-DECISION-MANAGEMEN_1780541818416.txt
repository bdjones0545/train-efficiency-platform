PHASE 19.5 — ORGANIZATIONAL GOVERNANCE & DECISION MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

Objective

Establish a formal governance framework that manages how strategic, operational, financial, workforce, and platform decisions are proposed, reviewed, approved, implemented, measured, and archived across the AI workforce.

Core Principle

Phase 19.1:
Communication

Phase 19.2:
Accountability

Phase 19.3:
Knowledge

Phase 19.4:
Standardization

Phase 19.5:
Governance

Governance determines who can make decisions, how decisions are approved, and how outcomes are measured.

The goal is to create a formal management system for the digital organization.
