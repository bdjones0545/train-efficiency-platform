Growth & Revenue Navigation Reorganization

Objective:
Reduce menu clutter, improve discoverability, and create a logical progression from lead generation → sales → revenue.

Current Problems:
- Outreach Center appears twice
- Athlete Leads and Athlete Lead Pipeline are difficult to distinguish
- Team Training Leads, Team Quotes, and Deal Pipeline are part of the same workflow but are separated
- Revenue tools are mixed with lead generation tools
- New users have no clear mental model for where to go

Proposed Navigation Structure

▼ Growth & Revenue

  ▼ Lead Generation (3)
      Athlete Leads
      Team Training Leads
      Outreach Center

  ▶ Sales Pipeline (3)
      Deal Pipeline
      Team Quotes
      Lead Pipeline

  ▶ Revenue Management (2)
      Transactions
      Redemptions

  ▶ Business Planning (1)
      Business Plan

Definitions

Lead Generation
Purpose:
Where opportunities enter the business.

Pages:
- Athlete Leads
- Team Training Leads
- Outreach Center

Sales Pipeline
Purpose:
Where opportunities are nurtured and converted.

Pages:
- Deal Pipeline
- Team Quotes
- Lead Pipeline

Revenue Management
Purpose:
Where money and payments are tracked.

Pages:
- Transactions
- Redemptions

Business Planning
Purpose:
Long-term business growth and forecasting.

Pages:
- Business Plan

Implementation Requirements

Sidebar
- Convert Growth & Revenue into a grouped accordion structure similar to AI Operations.
- Display item counts beside each group.
- Lead Generation expanded by default.
- Other groups collapsed by default.
- On mobile, only one subgroup can be open at a time.
- Persist expansion state in localStorage.

Cleanup
- Remove duplicate Outreach Center entry.
- Keep all routes unchanged.
- Navigation-only refactor; no backend changes.

Visual Consistency
Match AI Operations behavior:
- Group headers with icons
- Count badges
- Expand/collapse chevrons
- Active route highlighting
- Auto-expand correct group when navigating directly to a page

Additional Naming Audit

Review:
- Athlete Leads
- Athlete Lead Pipeline

Potential issue:
These names are extremely similar and may confuse users.

Recommendation:
Rename "Athlete Lead Pipeline" to one of:
- Lead Pipeline
- Sales Pipeline
- Athlete Sales Pipeline
- Conversions

Choose the option that best reflects the page's actual purpose.

Deliverables
- Files changed
- Navigation tree before/after
- Routes mapped to new groups
- Mobile verification
- Desktop verification
- Confirmation no routes, permissions, or APIs changed
