Phase 5 Features

1. Continuous Opportunity Monitoring

Run automatically.

Detect opportunities based on configurable rules.

Examples:

* Session below utilization threshold
* High-value session underfilled
* Session starts within 72 hours
* Waitlist pressure
* Coach cancellation
* Weather-related attendance risk
* Holiday schedules

Urgency

Probability of Filling

Overall Priority

94

Acceptance Criteria

* No automatic sending.
* Automatic opportunity detection.
* Automatic recipient ranking.
* Automatic draft generation.
* Human approval remains mandatory.
* AI recommendations are advisory.
* Existing AgentMail safety controls remain unchanged.
