PHASE 19.4 — ORGANIZATIONAL PROCEDURES & SOP OPERATING SYSTEM

Objective

Transform organizational knowledge into executable operational standards by creating a centralized Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) system that governs how work is performed, verified, measured, and continuously improved across the digital workforce.

Core Principle

Phase 19.1:
Communication

Phase 19.2:
Accountability

Phase 19.3:
Knowledge

Phase 19.4:
Standardization

Knowledge explains what works.

Procedures ensure it happens consistently.

The goal is to move from institutional memory to organizational execution standards.
