CEO Heartbeat UX + Mobile Fix

1. After pressing Initialize Heartbeat, the UI still shows first-run/no-run cards and requires more button presses.

3. Mobile layout is broken:

   - cards are too wide
   - buttons overflow inside cards
   - page allows sideways scrolling

  - text-sm
