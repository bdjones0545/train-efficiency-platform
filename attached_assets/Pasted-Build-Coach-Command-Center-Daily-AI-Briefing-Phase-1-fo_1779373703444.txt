Build Coach Command Center + Daily AI Briefing Phase 1 for TrainEfficiency.

GOAL:
Create a centralized AI-powered operational dashboard for coaches.

The system should synthesize:
- readiness
- workouts
- PRs
- interventions
- education
- streaks
- attendance
- missed sessions
- risk flags
- athlete engagement

into one actionable daily command center.

====================================================
1. NEW PAGE
====================================================

Create:
 /org/:slug/coach/command-center

This becomes the coach’s home base.

====================================================
2. DAILY AI BRIEFING
====================================================

Top hero section:
“Today’s AI Briefing”

Generated daily by GPT-4o.

AI receives:
- athlete status scores
- active risk flags
- readiness trends
- missed workouts
- PR spikes
- intervention queue
- education compliance
- upcoming sessions
- follow-ups due

AI returns:

A. Top Priorities
Examples:
- “3 athletes show declining recovery scores.”
- “Hydration compliance dropped among linemen.”
- “2 interventions require follow-up today.”

B. Positive Wins
- PR streaks
- education completions
- adherence improvements

C. Recommended Actions
- assign recovery pathway
- review athlete workload
- follow up with disengaged athletes

Tone:
professional coaching operations assistant.
