You are upgrading the TrainEfficiency system into a goal-driven business operator.

Current state:
- Fully adaptive system with client-level intelligence
- Autonomous execution (safe actions)
- Strategic recommendations (segmentation + LTV)

Goal:
Introduce GOAL-ORIENTED OPTIMIZATION

The system must:
- define targets
- track progress toward targets
- adjust decisions dynamically to hit targets

========================
PHASE 1 — WEEKLY TARGET SYSTEM
========================

Create:
set_weekly_targets

Targets may include:
- revenue target (e.g. $5,000/week)
- sessions target (e.g. 60 sessions)
- retention target (e.g. 90% active clients retained)
- utilization target (e.g. 80% capacity)

Store:
weeklyTargets

Create:
get_weekly_progress

Returns:
- current progress vs target
- gap remaining
- % complete
- projected outcome

Agent must answer:
- “Am I on track this week?”
- “How far am I from my goal?”

========================
PHASE 2 — GOAL-AWARE DECISION ENGINE
========================

Modify action scoring:

Add:
goalPriorityWeight

If revenue behind target:
→ boost high-revenue actions (upsell, backfill)

If retention dropping:
→ boost churn recovery

If utilization low:
→ boost slot filling

New score:
goalAdjustedScore =
clientAdjustedScore × goalPriorityWeight

Agent must say:
“Prioritizing this because you're behind your revenue target.”

========================
PHASE 3 — DYNAMIC STRATEGY ADJUSTMENT
========================

Update:
get_strategic_recommendations

Now must include:
- current goal status
- biggest gap (revenue / retention / utilization)
- strategy shift recommendation

Examples:
- “You are behind revenue → focus on upsell + backfill”
- “Retention is dropping → focus on churn recovery”
- “Utilization is low → prioritize filling open slots”

========================
PHASE 4 — DAILY GOAL TRACKING
========================

Extend:
get_daily_action_queue

Add:
- contribution toward weekly goals
- expected impact per action

Example:
“This action contributes ~$70 toward your $500 weekly gap.”

========================
PHASE 5 — GOAL COMPLETION FEEDBACK
========================

Create:
get_goal_performance_summary

At end of week:
- targets vs actual
- what actions contributed most
- what strategy worked best
- what to change next week

Agent must answer:
- “Did I hit my goals?”
- “What helped me hit (or miss) them?”

========================
PHASE 6 — AUTO-ADJUSTMENT
========================

If system detects:
- falling behind targets

Then:
- increase automation intensity (within safety)
- prioritize higher ROI actions
- recommend more aggressive strategy

========================
PHASE 7 — VALIDATION
========================

Test:

1. “Set a $5,000 weekly revenue goal”
2. “Am I on track this week?”
3. “What should I do to hit my goal?”
4. “How far behind am I?”
5. “What actions contribute most to my goal?”
6. “Did I hit my goal this week?”
7. “What should I change next week?”

========================
FINAL RULE
========================

The system must evolve from:
strategy → execution → optimization

into:
GOAL → STRATEGY → EXECUTION → OPTIMIZATION

Every action must now connect to a goal.
Every recommendation must reflect goal progress.
