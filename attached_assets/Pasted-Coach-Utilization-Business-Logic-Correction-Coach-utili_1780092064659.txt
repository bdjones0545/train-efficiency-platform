Coach Utilization Business Logic Correction

Coach utilization should represent:

Scheduled Capacity Utilization = Scheduled Session Time ÷ Available Coach Time

The current implementation appears to be measuring availability incorrectly and producing misleading results.

Requirements:

Utilization Formula

If availability blocks exist:

Utilization % =
(Booked Minutes This Week ÷ Available Minutes This Week) × 100

Only include:

* Confirmed bookings
* Completed bookings
* Current week

Exclude:

* Cancelled bookings
* No-shows
* Draft bookings

⸻

No Availability Blocks Scenario

If a coach has bookings but no availability blocks:

Do NOT show:

0% Utilization

Instead show:

Active
X sessions this week
No availability schedule configured

Reason:

A coach cannot logically be 0% utilized if they are actively delivering sessions.

⸻

Open Slots Logic

Current open slot calculation appears inflated because it includes the entire week’s recurring template.

Replace with:

Future Open Slots =
Future Available Capacity
−
Future Confirmed Bookings

Only count:

* Future availability
* Remaining days in the week

Do NOT count:

* Past unfilled availability
* Expired availability blocks
* Historical unused hours

Example:

If Hunter has:

20 future available hours
5 future booked hours

Display:

15 Open Slots
25% Utilization

Not:

80 Open Slots
7% Utilization

based on the full weekly template.
