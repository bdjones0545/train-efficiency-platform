Phase 10 — Executive Intelligence & Autonomous Recommendations

Goal:
Transform Opportunity Acquisition from a learning system into an executive decision support system.

Current State:

Discovery
→ Qualification
→ Outreach
→ Execution
→ Replies
→ Outcomes
→ Learning
→ Hermes Insights

New State:

Discovery
→ Qualification
→ Outreach
→ Execution
→ Replies
→ Outcomes
→ Learning
→ Executive Intelligence
→ Actionable Recommendations

Important:

Do NOT automatically modify system behavior.

Do NOT automatically change discovery weights.

Do NOT automatically alter outreach messaging.

Do NOT automatically send outreach.

Executive Intelligence should recommend actions, not execute them.

Human approval remains required.

Examples:

“Send 3 approved drafts awaiting outreach.”

“Follow up with 4 interested opportunities.”

“Address declining reply rates in consulting opportunities.”
