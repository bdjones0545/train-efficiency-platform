Part 3 — Rule Effectiveness Scoring

Connect outcomes back to learning rules.

Create table:

agent_rule_effectiveness

Fields:

* id
* org_id
* rule_id
* communication_domain
* message_type
* times_applied
* sent_count
* reply_count
* meeting_count
* proposal_count
* conversion_count
* hired_count
* lost_count
* revenue_cents
* effectiveness_score
* last_calculated_at

Calculation:

* replies increase score
* meetings increase score more
* conversions/contracts/hired increase score most
* ignored/lost outcomes decrease score
* repeated rejection decreases score

Example:
A rule that gets approvals but no replies should not keep gaining weight.
A rule that produces meetings or revenue should gain weight.

Part 6 — Autonomy Upgrade

Autonomy promotion should now require outcome proof, not just approval proof.

For each communication_domain + message_type:

Level 0 — Manual Approval
Default.

Level 1 — Suggested
Drafts only.

Level 2 — Auto-send Low Risk
Requires:

* 20+ reviewed messages
* 90% approval rate
* rejection rate <= 5%
* rule compliance >= 90%
* repeated mistakes below threshold
* reply rate above baseline OR booking/meeting outcomes present

Level 3 — Autonomous With Monitoring
Requires:

* 50+ reviewed messages
* 30+ sent messages
* positive outcome score
* no recent safety issues
* admin explicitly enables it

Do not promote autonomy based only on approvals.
