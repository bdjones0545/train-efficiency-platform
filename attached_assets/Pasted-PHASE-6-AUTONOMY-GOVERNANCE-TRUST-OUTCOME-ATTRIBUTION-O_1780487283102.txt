PHASE 6 — AUTONOMY GOVERNANCE, TRUST & OUTCOME ATTRIBUTION

Objective

Transform TrainEfficiency from an autonomous operating system into a trusted autonomous operating system by proving ROI, explaining decisions, measuring outcomes, and providing complete executive control.

Core Principle

Autonomous Management:
Executes business improvements.

Trust & Attribution:
Proves the improvements worked.

Current Problem

The system can:

* Configure workforce
* Execute workflows
* Generate intelligence
* Run initiatives
* Manage objectives

But executives still need answers to:

* What exactly changed?
* Why was it changed?
* Who approved it?
* Did it improve the business?
* Can I undo it?

Phase 6 solves those questions.
