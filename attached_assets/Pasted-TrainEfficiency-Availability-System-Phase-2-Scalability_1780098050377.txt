TrainEfficiency Availability System — Phase 2 Scalability Redesign

Objective:
Replace the current form-based availability management system with a scheduling operating system that scales from 1 coach to hundreds of coaches, locations, and thousands of monthly bookings.

Guiding Principle:
Coaches do not think in terms of individual availability records.

Current mindset:
Day → Start Time → End Time → Location

Desired mindset:
Location → Schedule → Capacity → Revenue

The system should feel like Google Calendar, Calendly, and Mindbody combined.

SUCCESS CRITERIA
───────────────────────────────

A coach should be able to:

1. Set an entire week's availability in under 60 seconds.

2. Understand their utilization immediately.

3. See open revenue opportunities instantly.

4. Manage availability visually rather than through forms.

5. Scale from 1 coach to 100+ coaches without changing workflows.

This should evolve Availability from a settings page into a Capacity & Revenue Management System.
