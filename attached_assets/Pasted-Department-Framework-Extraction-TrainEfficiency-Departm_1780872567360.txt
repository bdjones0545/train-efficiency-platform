Department Framework Extraction — TrainEfficiency Department OS v1

Goal:
Extract the reusable architecture behind Opportunity Acquisition and turn it into a generalized Department Framework that any future AI-powered department can use.

This is not a new department.

This is a platform-level architectural refactor.

The objective is:

Build Once
Deploy Everywhere

Opportunity Acquisition becomes the first implementation of a reusable Department OS.
