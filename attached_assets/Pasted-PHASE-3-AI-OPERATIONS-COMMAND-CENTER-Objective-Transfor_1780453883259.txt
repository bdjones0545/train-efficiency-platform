PHASE 3 — AI OPERATIONS COMMAND CENTER

Objective

Transform AI Operations from a monitoring page into the live operational headquarters for the organization’s AI workforce.

Current State

The Workforce Management Dashboard now answers:

* Is my workforce configured?
* Is it healthy?
* What is missing?
* What should I improve?

The next layer should answer:

* What is happening right now?
* What are agents actively working on?
* What requires my attention?
* What business outcomes are being generated?

Core Principle

Workforce Dashboard = Strategy Layer

AI Operations = Execution Layer
